Nachos

This is a simple recipe for; nice, very cheesy, very spicy nachos.

Ingredients:

  • Cheddar cheese
  • Spicy tortilla crisps
  • Guacamole
  • Creme fra√Æche
  • Spicy tomato sauce
  • Big, fat, juicy, peppers

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 degrees Celsius.
  2. Get a big (preferably glass) square bowl.
  3. Put in the tortilla crisps.
  4. Put the slices cheddar cheese over the crisps.
  5. Put the peppers on the cheese.
  6. If the oven is ready put the bowl with the Ingredients inside.
  7. Wait for 10-20 minutes before taking the bowl out of the oven.
  8. Check if it is warm and if the cheese has melted.
  9. Finally just put the bowl on the dining table, let everyone take a small portion and let them put the sauces on their plate.

Congratulations!!! you made some delicious Nachos
